Vikas, a fashion design instructor, dances with students of the advanced class in a bus on the way to a presentation in Gwal Pahari, India. Students of NGO Adharshila help each other to make patterns for garments. Adharshila is an NGO based in New Delhi, India that aims to provide marketable skills like fashion design to less fortunate women.

Sunita, a student of the fashion design classes, poses for a photo with her father, who came to the Adharshila center to discuss Sunita's progress and options after taking the class in Delhi, India. Involving families in the process is important for encouraging them to stay in the program which aims to give women the skills to be independent earners.
