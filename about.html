I'm Amanda Allen, a photographer raised in Flint, MI and currently based in Chicago. (#dontforgetFlint)

I graduated from the University of Michigan in 2017, where I persued photojournalism at the Michigan Daily and was elected Managing Photo Editor for 2016.

My degree is in International Studies and Asian Languages and Cultures because I am interested in communication and inter-cultural exchange. There is so much we can learn from each other when the communication is not one-sided.

My favorite form of communication is art. There is more that goes into understanding than just words. Images, design, and technology all work together to form a message.

Other than photography, which I am always practicing, I am also currently working on learning more about User Experience Design and programming, and working on my Urdu skills.

I am available for freelance anywhere
